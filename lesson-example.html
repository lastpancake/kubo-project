About

Students will get familiar with KUBO and the TagTiles® by using their own body to act out the commands printed on the movement tiles. They will build an understanding of sequences and algorithms by laying down movement TagTiles® and programming KUBO to do the actions printed on them. In later exercises, they will explore functions.
